## How to Add Yourself

After your first merged PR, comment on your PR:

```
@all-contributors please add @<username> for <contribution types>
```

Contribution types: `code`, `doc`, `test`, `bug`, `ideas`, `review`, `security`,
`design`, `infra`, `fuzz`, `benchmark`, `question`, `maintenance`, `projectManagement`.

See [CONTRIBUTING.md Section 13](docs/CONTRIBUTING.md#13-recognition) for details.
